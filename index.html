Short History

Bandhgora Anchal (X-Class) Vidyalaya (H.S) is located at Gai-Chandra, P.O: Bandhgora, P.S/BL/Dist. Jhargram PIN: 721514. It was in 1964 when the Vidyalaya authority had gone from door to door in search of students. Now the situation has undergone a sea change. The torch-bearer of knowledge Sri Panchkari Dey , Ex MLA of Jhargram and Ex-Principal opf Sevayatan B.Ed College with the heart-felt help of the poor Villagers of the Bandhgora Anchal No.2 had decided unanimously to establish a Vidyalaya. In the first meeting with the Managing Committee of this Vidyalaya on 12/06/1964, Panchkari Dey, Balaram Sahu, Narendranath Satpathy, Harendranath das, Muchiram Mahata, Radha Ranjan Kar, Amulya Charan Mahakur and Rashbehari Chalak identified the site for the establishment of this Vidyalaya. The donors had donated 6 acres of land  for the Vidyalaya.
